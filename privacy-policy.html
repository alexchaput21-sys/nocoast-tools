Privacy Policy

No Coast Custom Upholstery LLC  ·  Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

No Coast Custom Upholstery LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is a custom upholstery shop located in Grand Forks, North Dakota. We can be reached at nocoastcustomupholstery@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you call our business phone number, we may collect your phone number for the purpose of following up on your inquiry. We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial data, social security numbers, or health information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used solely to send a single automated follow-up SMS message after you call our shop. This message contains a link to our quote request form and lets you know the owner will follow up within 24 hours.

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than responding to your inquiry.

4. SMS Messaging

If you have called our business, you may receive one automated text message from us at (701) 929-9575. Standard message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your phone number is only used internally to respond to your inquiry.

6. Data Retention

We retain your phone number only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. Once your job is complete or if you opt out, your number is removed from our follow-up system.

7. Your Rights

You may request that we delete your information at any time by contacting us at nocoastcustomupholstery@gmail.com or by replying STOP to any text message we send.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
